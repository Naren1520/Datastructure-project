Products Management

View, manage, and organize all your inventory items.

Accessing Products

  1. Click "Products" in the navigation bar
  2. Or click the "Manage Products" card on the dashboard

Features & Buttons

View Products
Displays all products in a table format with ID, Name, Price, and Quantity
Sort Options
Sort by ID: Arrange products by identification number
Sort by Name: Alphabetical arrangement
Sort by Price: Low to high pricing
Edit Product
Click the Edit button next to any product to modify its details (Name, Price, Quantity)
Delete Product
Click the Delete button to remove a product from inventory. This action cannot be undone.

Product Table Layout

Column Description
Product ID Unique identifier for each product
Product Name Name of the product
Price Current price per unit
Quantity Number of units in stock
Actions Edit or Delete buttons
Tip: Use Sort options to quickly organize products by your preferred criteria for easier management

Add New Product

Add new items to your inventory with a simple form.

How to Add a Product

  1. Click "Add Product" in the navigation bar
  2. Or click the "Add Product" card on the dashboard
  3. Fill in the required fields:
    • Product ID: Unique number (cannot be duplicate)
    • Product Name: Name of the item
    • Price: Cost per unit
    • Quantity: Number of units to add
  4. Click the "Add Product" button
  5. Confirmation message will appear if successful

Form Fields Explained

Product ID

A unique number to identify this product. Must be different from all other products. Cannot contain spaces or special characters.

Product Name

The name or description of your product. Can include spaces and special characters.

Price

The cost per unit. Enter numerical values only (e.g., 99.99)

Quantity

How many units to add to inventory. Must be a whole number.

Important: Product IDs must be unique. If you try to add a product with an existing ID, the system will reject it.

Search Products

Quickly find products by their ID.

How to Search

  1. Click "Search" in the navigation bar
  2. Or click the "Search Products" card on the dashboard
  3. Enter the Product ID you want to find
  4. Click the "Search" button
  5. Results will display if the product exists

Search Results

If the product is found, you'll see:

  • Product ID
  • Product Name
  • Price
  • Current Quantity
  • Sell/Update options

Available Actions

Sell
Decrease quantity by 1 unit (for selling/using a product)
Update
Modify the product's name, price, or quantity
Tip: Search is the fastest way to find a specific product when you know its ID

? Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit a product after adding it?

Yes! Go to Products, click Edit next to any product, make changes, and save.

What happens if I delete a product?

The product is permanently removed from inventory. Be careful when deleting!

How do I sell a product?

Use the Search feature, find the product, and click "Sell" to decrease quantity by 1.

Can I sort products?

Yes! In Products view, use Sort buttons to arrange by ID, Name, or Price.

How do I track rentals?

Go to Rentals, click "Record Rental", fill in customer and product details, then save.

Where can I see sales analytics?

Click Analytics to see charts, trends, and statistical summaries.

Can I customize the theme?

Yes! Go to Settings and choose your preferred theme.

How do I understand the code?

Use the Developer Tools - Code Viewer, Documentation, and Flow Diagrams will help!
